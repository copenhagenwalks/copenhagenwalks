Make your group visit, birthday or special occasion in Copenhagen a memorable one with a visit by Denmark's world-famous poet and fairytale writer, Hans Christian Andersen. The poet will arrive at the restaurant or location of your choice in his 19th-century costume for an English performance in which he tells about his life and work as well as acting out one of his famous fairytales.

Performances can be arranged from May thru September and cost 180 US dollars. For more information and reservations you may contact copenhagenwalks@yahoo.com.
